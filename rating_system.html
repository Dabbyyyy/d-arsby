The Rating System

10
Undisputed Masterpiece. Not perfect by any means, but there's nothing I'd change about it.
9
A Flawed Masterpiece. A few things I'd do differently, but a masterwork nonetheless.
8
The Standard. If I were to create a piece of media, I'd want it to be rated 8/10.
7
Good. A fun experience overall and a worthwhile pastime.
6
A Passing Grade. Disposable media I'd consume and forget.
5
Average. Nothing special or worth seeking out.
4
Below Average. Not bad enough to be rated a 3, but not good enough to be rated a 5.
3
Bad. The world of entertainment is better off without this piece of media.
2
Very Bad. An insult to the art to storytelling itself.
1
Unconsumable garbage.
X.5
Certain pieces of media will get a partial rating of (X.5). This means they fit in somewhere between 2 ratings – a single compelling arguement away from either (X+1) or (X-1)
